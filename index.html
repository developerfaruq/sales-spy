Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is your website & ecommerce leads database?

Our database is updated daily with real-time information from both websites and online stores. We employ a combination of AI algorithms and human verification to ensure 98.5% accuracy of all lead information, including traffic data, technology stack, and revenue metrics. We also provide a data freshness indicator for each lead.

What makes your leads better than competitors?

Unlike generic lead databases, we focus exclusively on active websites and e-commerce businesses. This specialization allows us to provide deeper insights, more accurate data, and better targeting capabilities specifically for digital service providers and agencies.

Can I export the leads data?

Yes, you can export leads in various formats including CSV, Excel, and direct to your CRM. Our Pro and Enterprise plans also offer API access for seamless integration with your existing systems.

Do you offer a free trial?

Yes, we offer a 7-day free trial with access to 100 leads so you can test our platform before committing. No credit card required to start your trial.

How often is the data updated?

Our database is updated daily with new leads and refreshed information. Enterprise customers receive real-time updates as new data becomes available.

Is there a limit on how many leads I can access?

Each plan has specific limits on monthly lead access. Basic plan includes 500 leads per month, Pro plan includes 2,000 leads per month, and Enterprise plans offer unlimited access. Unused leads do not roll over to the next month.
